# Badge Art Prompts — Image Generation Reference

Prompts designed for **Nano Banana** and **GPT Image 1** generators.
Each badge group uses a distinct visual style to make categories instantly recognizable.

**Global instructions to prepend to every prompt:**
> Square format, 512×512px, badge/icon design on transparent or solid dark background, centered composition, no text, no letters, no numbers, clean edges suitable for circular or rounded-square crop.

## Generation Tips

1. **Nano Banana**: Works best with shorter, more descriptive prompts. Consider trimming the atmospheric descriptions and focusing on the key subject + style keywords.
2. **GPT Image 1**: Handles longer, more detailed prompts well. Use the full prompts as written.
3. **Consistency**: Generate all badges within a group in the same session/seed range to maintain style consistency.
4. **Post-processing**: All badges will need to be cropped to consistent dimensions (likely 256×256 or 512×512) and may need background removal for the ring overlay system.
5. **Ring compositing**: The 6 velocity streak rings are designed as transparent overlays. Generate them with explicit "transparent background" or "alpha channel" instructions, then composite in Figma/Photoshop.
